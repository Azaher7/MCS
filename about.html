Welcome to MCS

Our Journey to Excellence

At MCS, we are driven by innovation, excellence, and the ambition to shape the future of construction in the UAE. With a dedication to delivering high-quality projects, we aim to meet the evolving demands of our clients, partners, and the communities we serve.

MCS has steadily evolved into a regional leader. Starting from humble beginnings in Al Ain in 1977, we have expanded our expertise across commercial, residential, institutional, and industrial construction. We consistently deliver exceptional value to our clients and partners.

Our growth is driven by a relentless commitment to quality, sustainability, and innovation, which has helped us set new benchmarks in every project we undertake.

MCS was established in 1977 in Al Ain, UAE. With over 45 years of experience, we have grown from a general contractor into a comprehensive construction firm serving clients across Abu Dhabi, Dubai, and Al Ain.

We serve multiple sectors including commercial (offices, retail, hospitality), residential (villas, compounds, apartments), educational (schools, institutions), and industrial (oil & gas, infrastructure, storage facilities).

Safety is integral to everything we do. Our safety program exceeds the industry average by 90%. We are OSHAD-SF approved and ISO 45001 certified, with full integration of safety planning into construction scheduling.
