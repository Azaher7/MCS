About MCS

Building the UAE's Future Since 1977

MCS General Contracting and Maintenance, LLC (formerly Bin Maktoum General Contracting Company) is a UAE-based construction firm delivering comprehensive building solutions across commercial, residential, hospitality, educational, and industrial sectors.

Headquartered in Abu Dhabi with operations spanning Dubai and Al Ain, we combine decades of experience with modern construction practices to deliver projects that exceed expectations.

Offices across Abu Dhabi, Dubai & Al Ain

MCS provides comprehensive construction services across commercial (office towers, retail, hotels), residential (villas, compounds, apartments), educational facilities, institutional buildings, and industrial/infrastructure projects including oil & gas facilities and storage tanks.

MCS operates across the UAE with offices in Abu Dhabi (headquarters), Dubai, and Al Ain. We serve clients throughout the Emirates and have completed projects in all major cities.

MCS is ISO 9001:2015 (Quality), ISO 14001:2015 (Environmental), and ISO 45001:2018 (Health & Safety) certified. We are also OSHAD-SF approved and registered as an ADCE vendor with Abu Dhabi Chamber membership.
